Government Agriculture Schemes in India

1. Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Aims to improve irrigation and ensure water efficiency. “More crop per drop.”

Official Website

2. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Crop insurance scheme providing financial support in case of crop failure due to natural calamities.

Official Website

3. Soil Health Card Scheme

Promotes balanced fertilizer use by providing farmers with soil health reports and recommendations.

Official Website

4. Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Provides timely access to credit for farmers to meet their agricultural and allied needs.

Official Info (Vikaspedia)

5. e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)

Online trading platform for agricultural commodities to create a unified national market for farmers.

Official Website

6. Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Promotes organic farming through cluster-based approach for sustainable agriculture.

Official Website

7. National Food Security Mission (NFSM)

Enhances production of rice, wheat, pulses, and coarse cereals through area expansion and productivity improvement.

Official Website

8. Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Provides funds to states for development of agriculture and allied sectors with focus on improving productivity.

Official Website

9. National Agriculture Insurance Scheme (NAIS)

Crop insurance scheme offering financial support against crop loss due to natural calamities.

Official Website

10. Micro Irrigation Fund (MIF)

Encourages micro irrigation for efficient water use through financial support to states and farmers.

Official Website

11. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Provides income support of ₹6,000 per year directly to farmers’ accounts in three installments.

Official Website

12. National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

Focuses on climate-resilient agriculture and water use efficiency for sustainable farming.

Official Website

13. Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Provides financial support for post-harvest management infrastructure and agriculture marketing facilities.

Official Website

14. National Bee Board (NBB)

Promotes beekeeping and allied activities to increase farmers’ income.

Official Website

15. Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Supports organic farming to promote chemical-free farming methods.

Official Website

16. PM-AASHA (Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay SanraksHan Abhiyan)

Ensures remunerative prices to farmers for their produce to safeguard their interests.

Official Website

17. Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS)

Supports dairy farmers with subsidies for setting up dairy units and entrepreneurship.

Official Website

18. National Horticulture Mission (NHM)

Promotes holistic growth of horticulture sector including fruits, vegetables, flowers, and spices.

Official Website

19. Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)

Boosts fish production and aquaculture for doubling fishers' income.

Official Website

20. National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) Schemes

Supports cooperatives in agriculture, dairy, and allied sectors through financial assistance.

Official Website
